Zika Virus NS3 Protein Inhibitor Identification System

Computational BiologyDrug Discovery Molecular DockingBioinformatics

Objective

Identify potential inhibitors targeting the NS3 protein of the Zika virus through computational drug discovery.

Tools & Methods

  • AutoDock Vina, PyMOL, Open Babel — Virtual screening, molecular docking, and protein–ligand interaction analysis
  • SwissADME & pkCSM — ADME and drug-likeness analysis to evaluate bioavailability, toxicity, and pharmacokinetic properties
  • GROMACS — Molecular Dynamics (MD) simulations; analyzed RMSD, RMSF, hydrogen bonding, and structural stability

Outcomes

Successfully identified antiviral lead molecules through comprehensive computational screening and MD simulation analysis, contributing to potential therapeutic development against Zika virus.
